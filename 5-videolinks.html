Video Links


As a self-taught artist, I often turn to YouTube for instruction on technique, product reviews, and inspiration. These artists provide a wealth of current, relevant information that often isn't available in books. Here are a few links to some of my favorite YouTube artists and their channels.


Watercolor


Emily Olson and Louise De Masi's YouTube channels are a fantastic resource for watercolor art tutorials, demonstrations, and product reviews. I find their friendly, optimistic approach to painting highly motivating and enjoyable to watch.




Emily Olson



Louise De Masi




Pastel


Whenever I'm looking for step-by-step technique demonstrations in pastel, I find myself consistently referring back to YouTube demonstrations by pastel artists Emma Colbert and Jason Morgan. Both of their channels contain an abundance of art tutorials, demonstrations, and product reviews.






Emma Colbert



Jason Morgan
